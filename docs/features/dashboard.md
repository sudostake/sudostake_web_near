# Dashboard workspace

## TL;DR
- Route: `/dashboard`
- This is the signed-in home for your account.
- It combines your vaults, lender positions, and wallet balances in one workspace.

## What you can do here
- Click `New vault` to open the vault creation dialog.
- Switch between `Vaults` and `Positions`.
- Open any listed vault to move into its detail page.
- Review your wallet NEAR and USDC balances.

## What you should expect
- The `Vaults` tab lists vaults you own.
- The `Positions` tab lists vaults where you are the accepted lender.
- Empty states appear when you have no vaults or no positions yet.

## What to do next
- Start the borrower path with [Create a vault](../guides/create-vault.md).
- Review the vault detail screen in [Vault page](./vaults.md).
- If you are lending, jump to [Lender positions](./lender-positions.md).
