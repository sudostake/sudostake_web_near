# Landing page

## TL;DR
- Route: `/`
- This is the public starting screen for the app.
- From here you can connect a wallet, open Discover, or inspect live request previews when data is available.

## What you can do here
- Click `Connect wallet` to open Wallet Selector.
- Click `Discover` to browse open requests without signing in.
- Open a live opportunity card to jump straight to a vault page.

## What this page does not do
- It does not show dashboard tools.
- It does not let you create a vault or fund a request directly.
- State-changing actions still happen after you move into dashboard or a vault page.

## What to do next
- If you want to browse first, open [Discover marketplace](./discover.md).
- If you want to borrow, connect your wallet and then open [Dashboard workspace](./dashboard.md).
- If you want to understand the wallet prompt first, read [Connect your wallet](./authentication.md).
